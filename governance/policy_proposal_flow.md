# Policy Proposal Flow

Date: 2026-03-16
Status: active

## Intent

Define how policy proposals are evaluated before policy files are edited.

## Required Sequence

- Evaluate proposal strengths.
- Evaluate proposal weaknesses and likely tradeoffs.
- Provide a clear recommendation with rationale.
- Ask for explicit user confirmation before editing policy files.
- Do not apply policy proposal edits until user confirmation is received.
